Honey Feed

UAEBee's bee syrup probably remains the safest and most reliable nectar substitute for honey bees. The concentration and quantity are equally important

Like a Flower

The ideal composition of the sugar spectrum is similar to that of flower honey and allows the bees to make optimal use of this feeding syrup

High Quality

UAEBee syrup is ready to use and easy to use. The high resistance to microbiological spoilage due to a sugar content of 72.7% guarantees a long shelf life.

Pure

contains only sugars that the bee can fully utilize, unlike comparable bee feed syrups based on wheat or corn.

Cost-effective

Cost-effective commercial supplements that better mimic the unique properties of natural plants.
